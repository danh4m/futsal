Futsal is a fast paced association football game played on a hard court, smaller than a football pitch, and mainly indoors. Joining Napier Futsal is a a great way to learn and understand this beautiful, fast-paced game whilst meeting fellow students and making new friends

SOME RULES
Futsal has similarities to five-a-side football however Futsal has it's own unique set of rules. Find out more from this QUICK GUIDE from UEFA.

To list a few...

- There are no throw-ins, when the ball goes out of play you can restart with a Kick-in
- Teams are composed of 1 goalie and 4 outfield players however squads are made up of 14 people.
- Five fouls = One Penalty.
- Unlimited substitutions - yep that's right - unlimited.


The Napier Futsal team is one for all skill levels and we welcome all levels of ability from complete beginner to more experienced players.

If you wish to join or have any queries then please contact: napierfutsalteam@gmail.com

WHO ARE WE?

Written in March 2022
Image

EST 2021

Formed in the late stages of 2021, Edinburgh Napier Futsal Club are currently one of the universities newest sports clubs. At the present time, we are only attending training and playing recreational games. This is because the league has finished for the season.

As a club we aim to participate in competitive futsal next season around September 2022.

TRAINING
Training currently takes place on a weekly basis every Wednesday evening at [EN]GAGE Sport and Fitness Centre from 20:15 until 21:45. Training takes place in the form of warm up stretches followed by drills, perhaps a Rondo or two before finally playing a Futsal game where we aim to implement what we have just trained.

If you wish to join or have any queries then please contact: napierfutsalteam@gmail.com
